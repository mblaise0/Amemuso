Amemuso Children Choir, AMEMUSITO

AMEMUSO in cooperation with other organizations has decided to establish a children's choir. Offering musical training to children from an early age is an important part of AMEMUSO's efforts to create a foundation for its long-term objective.

  • Our Vision

    To create an enabling environment that will foster and afford the exchange of knowledge between musical experts, budding choristers, talents, and enthusiasts to promote the true understanding of musical theory and history, and solfeggio.

  • Our Mission

    Amemuso is keen to promote the wealth of performing arts and the vital role they play in our community's culture, economy and quality of life.To establish the first musical conservatory in Abuja, with a goal to build long-lasting structures and to create a framework necessary for the professional training of Nigerian Music Artists and Choristers

  • Our Goals
    • To present world-class performance to diverse audiences
    • To promote Nigeria's best artist
    • To serve as a revitalization engine for the city of Abuja and Nigeria.

Welcome To Abuja Metroplitan Music Society

Abuja Metropolitan Music Society (AMEMUSO) choir an Opera group of talented singers from different works of life, religious background and ethnic group; with passion and sole aim of making and singing music. AMEMUSO has transformed the Abuja music and entertainment landscape with the launch of its "Opera Abuja" in Nov, 2007. The very successful concert marked the first steps towards realizing AMEMUSO's objectives which includes establishing a series of opera concerts which offer talented Nigerian musicians a platform to exhibit their art; Offering musical training to children from an early age as a means of building on its longer term objectives. and establishing the 1st music conservatory in Abuja in order to build long lasting structures necessary for the professional training of Nigerian musicians.
