Friends of AMEMUSO

Friends of AMEMUSO are expexted to play avital role in the AMEMUSO community and help to ensure excellence in the performing arts for years to come. Membership contributions provide support for all activities of the Music Schoolincluding student financial support, faculty, advancement, academic programs, community outreach and public performances) as well as Cultural Center.

Membership And Patron Groups

Become part of the AMEMUSO community by joining one of the vital memberships that support the Society's educational and performance activities. In return , you will recieve a variety of special benefits - and the knowledge that you are helping to foster the artistic growth of gifted children and adults in Nigeria.

Sponsorship

Sponsorships at APAC may include various rights and benefits, according to the level of support. We are more than happy to work with each sponsor to develop a customized, targeted Sponsorship package with your buisness objectives in mind.

Partnerships with APAC will be fully integrated, and will involve both traditional and non-traditional assests and benefits. Consider the value of branding with curious, active and engaged audiences, onsite visibility, media exposure, year-round recognition, online presence, and newtworking with potential clients.
