AMEMUSO Children Choir

AMEMUSO in cooperation with other organizations has decided to establish a children's choir. Offering musical training to children from an early age is an important part of AMEMUSO's efforts to create a foundation for its long-term objective. This program hopes make the dream come true that talented children from different social levels will have the opportunity to join the first major children's choir of the federal capital of Nigeria. Their vocal and musical training programme will be the responsibility of talented and qualified members of AMEMUSO choir and its artistical director, Mrs Cecilia Toledo, professional opera singer and wife of the German Ambassador in Nigeria. At the end of every year they should show the results of this programme through a public concert/performance, which will be part of the official program of OPERABUJA every year.
