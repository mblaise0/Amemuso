About AMEMUSO

Under the leadership of Mrs. Maria Cecilia Toledo, the Abuja Metropolitan Music Society (AMEMUSO) was established in 2006 to provide an avenue for Nigerians, especially children to develop their carieers as singers, voilinists, pianists and learn about every other aspect and discipline that has to do with music.

Abuja Metropolitan Music Society, is passionately committed to inspiring and enlightening audience with some of the most commanding voices in music. The ensemble has experience performing at numerous venues and events within Nigeria and international bodies. They are driven by the desire to attain international standards and believe strongly that their example will drive the interest to build a music school for talented Nigerians in Abuja. Due to their brilliant performance and increasing vocal levels, they have received invitations to perform in the USA and Germany.

Mrs Maria Cecilia Toledo de Schmillen, founder and artistic director, together with Mr. Samuel Ezugwu(Music Director), Mr. Abednego Okafor (official Pianist) and Mr James Agahyande (Secretary), lead the formation of the Abuja Metroplitan Music Society (AMEMUSO Choir) and AMEMUSO children choir (AMEMUSITO - directed by Joshua Opara), the end result isthe renowned annual performance of a Nigerian first - Operabuja.

After the success of the 2007 edition of Operabuja an in recognition of the Choir ÃÅs dedication, the FCT Minister donated a plot of land to AMEMUSO for the purpose of making their dream of a music complex in Abuja a dream come true. In October 2008, the Choir received training for two weeks from a German Master in vocal training, Mrs. Carmen Maria Anhorn. The AMEMUSO Master classes in 2008 were made possible through the support of the German Embassy and Lufthansa Airline. Abuja Metropolitan Music Society embodies the choir spirit, and invites musical talented individuals on their vibrant musical journey. Contact the choir for more information

Code of Ethics

Our Vision

To create an enabling environment that will foster and afford the exchange of knowledge between musical experts, budding choristers, talents, and enthusiasts to promote the true understanding of musical theory and history, and solfeggio.

Our Mission

Amemuso is keen to promote the wealth of performing arts and the vital role they play in our community's culture, economy and quality of life.To establish the first musical conservatory in Abuja, with a goal to build long-lasting struct and to create a framework necessary for the professional training of Nigerian Music Artists and Choristers

Goals of AMEMUSO

  • To present world-class performance to diverse audiences.
  • To provide arts eduaction opportunities for children and adults alike.
  • To bring diverse communities together through the universality of the arts.
  • To serve as a reveitalization engine for the city of Abuja and Nigeria
  • To improve the reputation and image of Nigeria and contribute to a new sense of community pride.
  • To establish a self sustaining musical institution that will provide performances and eduational opportunity
Our Programmes

Artistic Program

  • Opera, Symphony, Ballet, Pop, Jazz, Country, Gospel, Folk, Theatre and more! APAC will present an amazing range of programming under one gloriousroof.
  • Under a director, our resident companies - AMEMUSO choir and AMEMUSITO - will perform revenue generating international Operas, Concerts, national/ international workshops, art exhibitions, theater performances, etc.

Cultural Center

  • Under a Director, work together with well-established cultural centres in Nigeria under the same building to deepen cultural ties and plan the annual artistic program for the theatre;
  • Develop and support a plan and crieteria for awarding scholaships to talented young Nigeria musicians and for honorariums for visiting international teachers;
  • Activate internationalpartnerships with foreign music school by the cultural centre and its partners.

Musical School

  • Under the supervision of Amemuso, become an enterprise capable of earning money to support itself with participation of local and international partners.
  • Bring teachers from all over the world to train Nigerian teachers everyt three months( the Nigerian teachers will work at the Music school the whole year).
  • Assigned teachers will provide all students , at least, the basic tools of Music (reading and writing music, Solfeggio, piano, voilin, vocal training, music history and above all, the protection, love and knowledege of nature.
  • Annually call for public auditions for talented young musicians. At least one or more scholarships will be awarded per year.
  • Embrace the challenge of developing a strong structure according the formal curriculums and models of other well-known music schoolsnof the world.
  • Through international agreements, secure an international official status. After completing 10years of studies, AMEMUSO students should have the capacity to be accepted into international music schools (like The Juilliard School) to contine their music sudies.
